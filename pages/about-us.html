About us

We are a group of machine translation researchers aiming to provide highest quality MTL novels for passionate readers. To those who are interested, machine translation for novels is a difficult problem. One needs to analyze the source language carefully in order to deal with slang, jokes, ellipsis, pronouns and other complex language phenomenons. Instead of relying on Google/DeepL service, we believe our designated model can do the job better than anyone else worldwide. We are constantly making research progress for our large-scale translation model so that it makes fewer mistakes, and you can enjoy reading with a higher quality version. This takes time and computational resources. Your support is the greatest energy source for us! If you have any questions or suggestions for us, you might email us at support@Webnovels.com
